Our Mission

At Phoenician Game Studios, our mission is to transform workplaces and environments into vibrant, engaging, and enjoyable spaces. We are dedicated to pioneering innovative gamification solutions that foster a culture of motivation, collaboration, and productivity. Our commitment is to empower organizations with the tools and strategies they need to not only attract and retain top talent but also to cultivate an atmosphere where every employee thrives. We believe that work should be more than a duty; it should be a source of inspiration, creativity, and personal growth. Our vision is to revolutionize the world of work, one joyful interaction at a time.

Our founding

In 2019, inside a small studio apartment in Athens Greece, the idea of Phoenician Game Studios was concieved. A company that specializes in creating enjoyable workplaces to decrease the "I hate modays" mentality in employees and help employers create a sustainable workforce. A few years later, Phoenicians Game Studio came to fruition in Beirut Lebanon as the first Gamifier Consultancy in the MENA region.

Growth & beyond

From a small studio in Beirut, PGS has grown and is currently consulting clients in the Middle East, North Africa, Emirates, Saudi Arabia and Europe. No company is too big or too small for our services to make a difference as every client recieves a customised gamification package.
